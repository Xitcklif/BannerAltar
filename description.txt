BannerAltar - A mod that allows you to constantly get buffs from banners added to it, regardless of the player's distance from altar.
To work, the altar must be placed above the back.
Recipe:
- Ebonwood Table/Shadewood Table
- Honey Chair x2
- Bunny Banner
- Bird Banner
- Goldfish Banner

BannerAltar sources:
